What Is Virtual Assistance?

A Virtual Assistant (VA) is a remote professional who handles a wide range of administrative, communication, and coordination tasks on your behalf. Think of them as an extension of yourself — available to handle the work that keeps your business running so you can focus on the work that makes it grow.
